---
title: "Attack Context"
---

Each attack has a details page that gives you additional information on the
detected attack.

- When the attack started
- The last trace we detected as part of the attack
- A list of traces detected as part of the attack
- An estimated risk scores
  ![3808](/images/994e58a-96c77ed-Screenshot_2022-11-26_at_7.53.30_PM.png)

On the top right you can resolve the attack as either a false positive or a real
attack. If you mark the attack as a false positive our models take it into
consideration when generating future attacks.

### Installation

Metlo's Burp Suite Extension can be downloaded from [here](https://metlo-api-security-public.s3.us-west-2.amazonaws.com/metlo-burpsuite-plugin-latest.jar).

Install the Metlo's burp suite extension like usual. Instructions to do that can be found [here](https://portswigger.net/burp/documentation/desktop/extensions/installing-extensions).

Once installed, go the the `Metlo` tab, and configure the `METLO URL` and `API Key` fields and press `Save Config`.

![Configuring Metlo burp-suite extension](/images/screenshot_burp_suite_extension.png)

After saving the config, all requests/responses captured should be visible in the metlo tab.
![Configuring Metlo burp-suite extension](/images/screenshot_burp_suite_working.png)
